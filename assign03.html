Me in 30 Seconds

Hello everyone. My name is Darrin and I hail from Washington, Utah (just outside of St. George). My wife and I have been married for 13 years and have two girls, ages 7 and 3. I have spent the last 12 years as a jewelry designer and appraiser with a formal education in gemological studies from the Gemological Institute of America. As for my career goals; it is always wise to have a contingency plan. So, while I intend to spend my days making sparkling things, it is good to have a backup plan. This has led me to return to school to get a degree in technology. If sleeping in till 6:15 in the morning isn't a good hobby, I guess mine would be gardening. We have a half-acre with a small orchard, two vegetable gardens, three dozen rose bushes and a horde of additional flora. When we are not at home we are up in the mountains at the family cabin or down at Disneyland. These hobbies, coupled with family, church, work, and life, keeps me pretty busy. Most of my homework is done late at night, between 8-11 pm MST and I typically prefer to do all my homework on weekdays and save the weekends for family.
