Love/Hate

I have a love/hate relationship with gardening. We are lucky enough to have a yard with enough substance to allow for us to have a few garden plots, fruit trees, shade, and lots of grass. I love that we can experience nature just outside our door. The fruits and vegetables are fantastic. We have flowers year-round. All these things I love. I hate (yes, I know that is a strong word), hate, hate, weeds, and weeding. Our yard has some sort of superpower that enables weeds to ignore any weed treatment and act just like a hydra; you pull one and three more pop up.
